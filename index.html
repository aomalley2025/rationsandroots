Meet the Founders

We’re Freddy & Angel — a Veteran couple with a passion for real food and honest living.

Rations & Roots began in our own kitchen, born from the need for meals that are as convenient as they are clean.

What started as cooking for friends and family grew into a mission — to serve our community by providing something everyone needs: convenience.

There’s no better feeling than knowing we’ve made someone’s day easier, healthier, and a little more fulfilled.

Local ingredients. Real flavor. A labor of love that grew into something greater than we ever imagined.
